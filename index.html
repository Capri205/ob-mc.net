Welcome to ob-mc.net

Home of OB-Minecraft and PlayerServers

We have been slowly winding down our setup from a peak of 23 Minecraft servers and some CS:GO, Garry's Mod and Rust servers. We currently operate just the Minecraft build server and our popular 'PlayerServers' from the lobby. PlayerServers is where you can run your very own Minecraft server within our BungeeCord network, complete with a selection of plugins you can install. If you have a particular plugin you want installed or server configuration, then pop a message on our discord and we can help.

Go to our Discord channel for more information and the latest updates.
